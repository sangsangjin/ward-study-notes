OUTCOME OF CLINICAL PLACEMENT
Satisfactory
The student, with support that is commensurate to the student's level, has demonstrated an acceptable and safe standard of knowledge, skills and attributes, in relation to all seven (7) Practice Standards.
Unsatisfactory
The student has been unable to achieve an acceptable standard in one or more of the following Standards from the Nursing and Midwifery Board of Australia (NMBA) Registered Nurse Standards for Practice. Students who are a risk to patients, staff or themselves in the course of clinical placement may be withdrawn from the facility and deemed unsatisfactory.
